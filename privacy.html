Your Privacy Choices and Opt-Out Rights

We fully respect your rights regarding data privacy. You may manage, limit, or opt out of personalized ad tracking at any time through the following clear channels:

1. Device-Level Settings

You can reset your advertising ID or opt out of interest-based personalized advertising via your mobile device system settings at any time:

  • iOS Devices: Go to "Settings" > "Privacy & Security" > "Apple Advertising" / "Tracking," and turn off the option to allow apps to request to track, or limit personalized ads.
  • Android Devices: Go to "Settings" > "Google" > "Ads," and select "Delete advertising ID" or "Opt out of Ads Personalization."

2. GDPR Rights for EEA and UK Users

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or the United Kingdom, we will display a Consent Management Platform (CMP) pop-up when you first launch the app. You have the right to explicitly consent to or reject the use of your personal data for personalized advertising. You may also adjust or revoke your consent preferences at any time within the app's privacy settings or your device system settings.

3. CCPA Rights for California Consumers

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), users have the right to request that an app "Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information." In this application, the sharing of mobile advertising identifiers with third-party ad networks for interest-based advertising may be considered a "share" under the CCPA. You may exercise your right to opt out by following the steps outlined in the "Device-Level Settings" section above.
